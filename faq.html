Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about AottariRP, from getting started to troubleshooting technical issues

Whitelist applications can be found on our website under the "Apply for Whitelist" section. Fill out the application form with accurate information about yourself and your intended roleplay character. Make sure to read and understand the server rules before submitting your application. Once submitted, our team will review your application and get back to you via email/Disocrd.
To run AottariRP, you will need a PC with at least 8GB of RAM, a modern multi-core CPU, and a dedicated GPU with 4GB of VRAM. The game is optimized for Windows 10 and requires DirectX 11. Make sure your graphics drivers are up to date. Additionally, a stable internet connection is necessary for online gameplay. For the best experience, we strongly recommend using a gaming headset and a microphone for in-game communication. Application needed are Original GTAV Game , FiveM Client AND Discord for website whitelist applications.

To make a purchase, simply visit the Tebex store, browse the available items, and follow the checkout process. All transactions are secure and processed through trusted payment gateways.
