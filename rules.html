Community Conduct

Bullying
Any form of targeted harassment, intimidation, or belittling behavior directed at community members is strictly forbidden. We are committed to maintaining a respectful environment where all players feel welcome.
Penalty: Permanent ban
Discrimination
Aottari Roleplay has zero tolerance for discriminatory language or behavior. This includes but is not limited to racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, religious discrimination, or any form of hate speech.
Penalty: Permanent ban
OOC Abuse
Using out-of-character communications to verbally abuse or harass other players is prohibited. Maintain civility even when discussing disagreements.
Penalty: Warning or Temporary ban
Harassment
Threatening or harassing any individual or group within our community is strictly prohibited. In severe cases, we may collaborate with relevant authorities if legal boundaries are crossed.
Penalty: Permanent ban
Infrastructure Attacks
Any attempts to disrupt, damage, or gain unauthorized access to our servers, services, or infrastructure are strictly prohibited. This includes DDoS attacks, hacking, and any form of digital vandalism.
Penalty: Permanent ban
Discretionary Removal
We reserve the right to remove any content or user that we deem inappropriate or harmful to the community, at our sole discretion.
Penalty: Permanent ban
Common Sense
All members are expected to use common sense and good judgment when interacting with others. This includes being respectful, avoiding excessive negativity, and contributing positively to the community.
Penalty: Warning or Temporary ban
Encouraging Violations
Encouraging or inciting others to violate the rules is strictly prohibited. This includes promoting harmful behavior, sharing exploits, or any actions that undermine the integrity of the community.
Penalty: warning or Temporary ban
Age Falsification
Providing false information about one's age is strictly prohibited. This includes lying about age to bypass restrictions or to manipulate others.
Penalty: Permanent ban
Disruptive Behavior/Trolling
Engaging in disruptive behavior or trolling is strictly prohibited. This includes spamming, baiting, or any actions intended to provoke or annoy others.
Penalty: Warning or Temporary ban
Real Money Trading
Engaging in real money trading or the exchange of in-game items for real-world currency is strictly prohibited. This includes any form of account selling or trading.
Penalty: Permanent ban
Sensitive Content
Sharing or promoting sensitive content, including but not limited to hate speech, graphic violence, or explicit material, is strictly prohibited. In severe cases, we may collaborate with relevant authorities if legal boundaries are crossed.
Penalty: Permanent ban
AFK Policy
Players are expected to be active and engaged during gameplay. Prolonged inactivity (AFK) in critical areas may result in penalties.
Penalty: Warning
In-Game Disputes
Do not disrupt ongoing roleplay to address rule violations or disputes. Instead of threatening to report someone in-game, continue the roleplay and address concerns through proper channels afterward.
Penalty: Warning or Temporary ban
Ban Evasion
Attempting to circumvent a ban by creating new accounts or using alternative methods to access our servers will result in an extension of the original ban and permanent banning of any alternative accounts.
Penalty: Permanent ban

Exploitation & Cheating

Game Modifications
The use of any third-party software, scripts, or modifications that alter game functionality while connected to our servers is prohibited. This includes visual enhancement tools that could provide unfair advantages.
Penalty: Permanent ban
Exploiting Bugs
Intentionally using or attempting to use game bugs or glitches for personal advantage is forbidden. This includes item duplication, processing through walls, or bypassing intended game mechanics.
Penalty: Permanent ban
Combat Logging
Disconnecting from the server to avoid roleplay consequences is strictly prohibited. This includes but is not limited to logging out during combat, police pursuit, while detained, after death, or to preserve items/equipment.
Penalty: Permanent ban
Combat Storing
Using vehicle storage mechanics to gain unfair advantages during roleplay scenarios is prohibited. This includes but is not limited to storing vehicles to avoid pursuit or impounding/scrapping to prevent seizure.
Penalty: Warning or temporary ban
Improper Teleportation
Using the respawn function to travel across the map quickly or abusing garage systems to transport goods is prohibited. Respawn should only be used when legitimately killed or when stuck due to a game bug.
Penalty: Warning or temporary ban

Advertising & External Platforms

External Promotion
Promoting external platforms, servers, or communities within the server is prohibited without explicit permission from the staff team.
Penalty: permanent ban

Discord Conduct

Voice Chat Etiquette
Treat all players with respect both in-character and out-of-character. Harassment, discrimination, hate speech, or personal attacks will not be tolerated.
Penalty: Warning or temporary ban
Voice Modulation
The use of voice changers or soundboards in public or official channels (including emergency services rooms) is prohibited as it disrupts clear communication.
Penalty: warning
Audio Restrictions
Music and sound effects should only be played in designated music channels or private group channels. Playing audio in public or service channels disrupts communication.
Penalty: Warning
Appropriate Imagery
Profile pictures, avatars, and other visual representations must be appropriate and non-offensive. Graphic, explicit, or otherwise inappropriate imagery is not permitted.
Penalty: warning
Identity Misrepresentation
Impersonating other community members by adopting their usernames, avatars, or other identifying characteristics is prohibited. All decisions and instructions from server staff are final. Arguing with staff members in-game or on Discord will result in additional penalties.
Penalty: Warning or temporary ban

Forum Conduct

Forum Etiquette
Exercise maturity and respect when participating in forum discussions. Inappropriate behavior, including spamming, trolling, bullying, or using offensive language, is not permitted.
Penalty: Warning
Forum Profile Standards
Maintain appropriate forum profiles with suitable usernames and avatars that align with community standards.
Penalty: warning
Constructive Discussion
Creating threads or posts with the primary intention of causing controversy, arguments, or community division is prohibited.
Penalty: Warning or temporary ban
Report Non-Interference
Do not comment on player reports that do not involve you directly or in which you did not participate in the reported situation.
Penalty: Warning
Appeal Process Respect
Refrain from commenting on ban appeals unless you were directly involved in the situation leading to the ban.
Penalty: warning
Ban Discussion Guidelines
Discussions regarding bans should be limited to appropriate channels such as ban appeals. Using status updates or general forums to discuss ban-related matters is not permitted.
Penalty: warning

Staff Relations

Staff Decisions Are Final
Decisions made by the administration team are considered final after proper review. While constructive feedback is welcome, continuing to argue after a decision has been explained is unproductive.
Penalty: Warning
Staff Respect
Maintain respectful communication with staff members. Abusive language, aggressive behavior, or harassment directed at staff will not be tolerated.
Penalty: Permanent ban
Admin Observation
Staff members in-game will be identifiable by special uniforms when performing official duties. Please continue normal roleplay unless directly addressed by staff..
Penalty: warning
Staff Communication Boundaries
Please use official channels for staff communication rather than personal messages or social media, unless explicitly invited to do so by the staff member.
Penalty: Warning
Staff Accountability
Staff members found abusing their privileges or position will face appropriate disciplinary action, potentially including removal from the team and community bans.
Penalty: Permanent ban
Staff Assistance Limits
Staff members are prohibited from using administrative powers to provide players with items, money, or unfair advantages unless addressing a legitimate technical issue.
Penalty: warning
Staff Integrity
Staff members accepting bribes or payments in exchange for special treatment will be immediately removed from the team and banned. Players offering such bribes will also face permanent bans.
Penalty: Permanent ban
Confidentiality
Staff members must maintain confidentiality regarding internal discussions, upcoming features, or administrative decisions until officially announced to the communi
Penalty: Permanent ban
Staff Compliance
All community rules apply equally to staff members, with exceptions only permitted in specific situations requiring administrative intervention.
Penalty: warning

General Rules

Vehicle Combat (RVDM)
Using vehicles as weapons without legitimate roleplay justification is prohibited. This includes deliberately running over players, causing vehicle explosions, or ramming other vehicles outside of structured roleplay scenarios.
Penalty: permanent ban
Random Combat (RDM)
Engaging in combat without meaningful roleplay interaction is prohibited. Simply ordering someone to comply without context or using countdown ultimatums does not constitute sufficient roleplay justification for violence.
Penalty: permanent ban
Provocative Behavior
Deliberately creating situations to provoke hostile responses for the purpose of initiating combat is prohibited. This includes intentionally committing crimes in front of law enforcement or antagonizing groups known for aggressive responses.
Penalty: Warning or temporary ban
Chat Misuse
Flooding communication channels with excessive or irrelevant messages disrupts roleplay and community experience. Keep communications appropriate and relevant.
Penalty: Warning
Unrealistic Advantage
Creating scenarios where others have no reasonable chance to respond appropriately is prohibited. This includes roleplaying superhuman abilities, using game mechanics to trap players, or claiming unrealistic injuries to avoid consequences.
Penalty: Warning or temporary ban
Character Separation
Maintain clear boundaries between in-character and out-of-character interactions. Do not bring real-life disputes into roleplay or vice versa. Your character's actions and relationships are separate from your personal identity.
Penalty: Warning or temporary ban
Realistic Driving
Vehicle operation should reflect realistic limitations. Extreme stunts, surviving significant falls, or driving at excessive speeds in urban areas without acknowledging consequences breaks immersion. Accidents should be appropriately roleplayed.
Penalty: Warning or temporary ban
Leadership Responsibility
Players in leadership positions (business owners, faction leaders, senior emergency service personnel) are expected to exemplify excellent roleplay and adherence to community standards, serving as role models for other players.
Penalty: warning

Roleplay Rules

Voice Communication
A functioning microphone is required to participate in our roleplay server. Text-only communication significantly limits roleplaying capabilities and interaction quality.
Penalty: Warning
Language Requirements
English is our community's primary language for communication. While roleplaying characters with different linguistic backgrounds is permitted, please be prepared to communicate in English when necessary for clarity.
Penalty: warning
Immersive Roleplay
Remain in character during all interactions unless specifically directed otherwise by an administrator. Breaking character disrupts immersion for everyone involved.
Penalty: Warning or temporary ban
Self-Preservation
Your character should prioritize their own survival. When faced with significant threats or overwhelming opposition, compliance is generally more realistic than suicidal resistance.
Penalty: warning or temporary ban
OOC Purpose
Out-of-character chat should be used exclusively for necessary game-related assistance. Extended OOC conversations disrupt immersion and should be conducted through alternative platforms.
Penalty: Warning
OOC Information Sharing
Using OOC chat to broadcast in-game events or alert others to ongoing activities is prohibited as it circumvents normal roleplay communication channels.
Penalty: warning
Communication Limitations
If your character's communication devices are confiscated during roleplay, you must restrict communications accordingly. External voice platforms should not be used to circumvent in-game limitations.
Penalty: warning or temporary ban
Character Resources
Transferring money, items, or other resources between your own characters is strictly prohibited regardless of the method used.
Penalty: permanent ban
Property Ownership
Extended inactivity (exceeding one month) may result in the forfeiture of owned properties to ensure resources remain available to active community members.
Penalty: Warning
Consent for Intimate RP
Any roleplay involving sexual themes must have explicit out-of-character consent from all involved parties before initiation. Non-consensual sexual roleplay is strictly prohibited, and staff must be informed of consensual arrangements beforehand.
Penalty: permanent ban

Metagaming

External Information
Using information obtained outside of roleplay to influence in-game decisions is strictly prohibited. This includes details from streams, forums, Discord, or other external sources that your character would not legitimately know.
Penalty: Permanent ban
Player Identification
Identifying players by their session IDs rather than through roleplay recognition is prohibited. For uniformed personnel, you may reference visible rank insignia or collar numbers unless they are undercover or disguised.
Penalty: Warning/Temporary ban
External Communication
If using external communication platforms during roleplay, you must vocalize relevant information in-game when it would affect ongoing scenarios. This ensures all participants have access to the same information.
Penalty: Warning/Temporary ban
Social Media Integrity
In-game social platforms like Birdy are considered part of the roleplay environment. Sharing information that breaches New Life rules or represents out-of-character knowledge is prohibited.
Penalty: Warning/Temporary ban
Multi-Character Separation
Information learned through one character must not influence the actions of your other characters. Each character should operate with only the knowledge they have personally acquired through roleplay.
Penalty: Warning/Temporary ban

New Life Rule (NLR)

NLR Application
A new life begins when your character respawns after death in a roleplay scenario, with exceptions noted in NLR Exceptions. This reset means your character loses all memory of events leading to their death.
Type: Information
NLR Exceptions
The New Life Rule does not apply if a staff member has explicitly confirmed you were victim of clear RDM or RVDM. This confirmation must come through official channels such as the game server, forums, or communication platforms.
Type: Information
NLR Obligations
After respawning: Your character retains no memory of their death or preceding events; any conflicts with their killer(s) are considered resolved; relationships ended through death cannot be resumed; and you must avoid the location of your death for at least 15 minutes.
Penalty: Warning/Temporary ban
Incapacitation State
While incapacitated but before respawning, you are considered injured but not dead. Roleplay your injuries appropriately without referencing the respawn timer or other game mechanics.
Type: Information
Post-Revival Protocol
After receiving medical treatment, continue to roleplay your injuries appropriately. Do not immediately flee without roleplay justification. While you retain memories from before being downed, you must avoid combat situations for at least 15 minutes due to your injuries.
Penalty: Warning/Temporary ban

Character Development

Character Naming
Use realistic, original names for your character. Names referencing celebrities, fictional characters, historical figures, notorious criminals, or containing inappropriate wordplay are not permitted.
Penalty: Warning
Character Consistency
Maintain consistency in your character's personality, background, and appearance. Major character changes should be supported by appropriate roleplay development.
Type: Information
Permanent Character Death
Characters may be permanently removed from the server as part of roleplay scenarios only with mutual agreement between all involved parties and staff approval.
Type: Information
Destructive Characters
Characters created primarily to engage in widespread violence or disruptive behavior will face stricter consequences, and players may be required to use alternative characters.
Penalty: Warning/Temporary ban
Organization Naming
Names for businesses, gangs, and other organizations must be realistic and appropriate. Names that are legally problematic, inappropriate, or unrealistic will be removed without notice.
Penalty: Warning
Criminal Consequences
Persistent criminal activity will attract increased law enforcement attention, resulting in escalating penalties. In extreme cases, characters may face permanent imprisonment with administrative approval.
Type: Information
Progressive Development
Character progression should follow a realistic trajectory. New characters should not immediately possess extensive resources, influence, or criminal empires, but should gradually develop these through consistent roleplay.
Type: Information

Emergency Buildings

Facility Respect
Lingering around emergency service facilities (police stations, hospitals, fire stations) without legitimate roleplay purpose is prohibited. These areas should be treated as professional environments.
Penalty: Warning/Temporary ban

Combat & Hostages

Hostage Scenarios
Hostage situations must involve high-quality, immersive roleplay that creates an engaging experience for all participants. Focus on creating memorable interactions rather than mechanical outcomes.
Penalty: Warning/Temporary ban
Legitimate Hostages
Using friends, gang members, or willing participants as staged hostages is prohibited. Hostage situations should involve genuine roleplay with unaffiliated individuals.
Penalty: Warning/Temporary ban
Realistic Executions
Character executions must be conducted with appropriate realism and roleplaying quality. The method should match available tools (e.g., needing a bladed weapon for throat-cutting).
Penalty: Warning/Temporary ban
Threat Communication
Using communication systems to issue blanket threats against emergency services (e.g., "any police within X distance will be shot") is prohibited as it circumvents proper roleplay interaction.
Penalty: Warning/Temporary ban
Emergency Response
Police activating lights and sirens does not automatically constitute roleplay initiation. Attacking officers without proper roleplay interaction solely because they are responding to a scene is considered RDM.
Penalty: Warning/Temporary ban
Tactical Equipment
The deployment of tactical equipment like spike strips must be accompanied by proper roleplay interaction. Using such equipment without roleplay engagement is considered equivalent to RDM.
Penalty: Warning/Temporary ban
Character Conflicts
You may only have one character involved in any opposing faction at a time. If multiple characters could be engaged in conflicting storylines, you must choose only one to participate.
Penalty: Warning/Temporary ban

Police Rules

Police Equipment
Law enforcement officers must not willingly give away or sell their issued equipment except in compelling roleplay circumstances with strong justification.
Penalty: Warning/Temporary ban
Impartial Enforcement
Officers must enforce laws impartially regardless of personal relationships. Showing favoritism to friends or associates by reducing charges or overlooking violations without roleplay justification is prohibited.
Penalty: Warning/Temporary ban
Station Defense Exception
The 15-minute NLR restriction does not apply to police officers during police station attacks. Stations are considered permanently staffed with law enforcement personnel and monitored by security systems, making attacks particularly high-risk.
Type: Information
Police Integrity
Officers may not roleplay corrupt activities or behavior. This rule is enforced on a case-by-case basis by the administrative team and cannot be used as grounds for player reports.
Type: Information

Medical & Fire Rules

Medical Equipment Control
Medical and fire service personnel must not sell or willingly surrender their issued equipment to unauthorized individuals.
Penalty: Warning/Temporary ban
Balanced Response Coverage
Medical and fire personnel should maintain balanced coverage across the region rather than exclusively following specific groups or factions. Patrol assigned areas or the entire county to ensure equal service availability.
Penalty: Warning/Temporary ban
Emergency Services Protection
While medical and fire personnel may be targeted with appropriate roleplay justification if they refuse to cooperate with threats, they cannot be taken hostage for extended periods.
Penalty: Warning/Temporary ban
Impartial Care
Medical and fire personnel must not abuse their positions to provide special treatment or advantages to friends or associates outside of normal service protocols.
Penalty: Warning/Temporary ban
Unsafe Response Areas
Medical personnel must not attempt to provide care in areas where active combat is occurring. Patient rescue must wait until the scene is secure to maintain realism and safety protocols.
Penalty: Warning/Temporary ban
Emergency Vehicle Security
Taking or using emergency service vehicles without proper authorization from the respective department is prohibited.
Penalty: Warning/Temporary ban

Lost MC Rules

Alliance Limitations
The Lost MC may form temporary tactical alliances with other groups but must maintain their distinct identity and autonomy. Long-term non-aggression agreements are permitted.
Type: Information
Clubhouse Defense Exception
If the Lost MC clubhouse is attacked, Lost members are exempt from the standard 15-minute NLR timer and may immediately return to defend their territory.
Type: Information
Conflicting Allegiances
Players may not simultaneously maintain characters in both the Lost MC and 'The Firm' due to inherent conflicts of interest between these organizations.
Penalty: Warning/Temporary ban

Group & Gang Rules

Permanent Expulsion
If your character is killed as part of being removed from a group or organization, that character is permanently barred from rejoining that organization in the future.
Penalty: Warning/Temporary ban
Group Independence
Different groups and factions must operate independently during confrontations with other groups. Coordinating across group lines for activities such as combat assistance, information sharing, or simultaneous attacks is prohibited.
Penalty: Warning/Temporary ban
Limited Cooperation
Temporary alliances of up to 6 characters from different groups may form for specific roleplay scenarios. These coalitions are treated as separate entities and must still adhere to the coordination restrictions outlined in the zerg rule.
Type: Information
Conflict Resolution
Extended conflicts between groups will be subject to a two-week resolution period. If group leaders cannot reach an agreement within this timeframe, staff will intervene with impartial mediation based on community guidelines.
Type: Information
Wartime Recruitment Freeze
Groups engaged in active conflict may not recruit new members or expand their rosters until the conflict is resolved.
Penalty: Warning/Temporary ban
Group Transition Period
After leaving any whitelisted organization (voluntarily or otherwise), players must observe a 30-day cooling-off period before joining another whitelisted group to prevent metagaming and faction-hopping.
Type: Information
Engagement Cooldown
When connecting to the server, you must wait 15 minutes before involving yourself in any ongoing hostile situations related to your group or faction to prevent tactical logging and reinforcement abuse.
Penalty: Warning/Temporary ban
Character Exclusivity
A player may only have one character in any whitelisted group at any given time to prevent conflicts of interest and information sharing between competing organizations.
Type: Information

Important Notice

These rules are not exhaustive. Staff members have discretion to address situations not explicitly covered here. The primary goal is to maintain a quality roleplay environment for all players. When in doubt, prioritize realistic roleplay and common sense.

All bans can be appealed through our Discord server. Repeat offenders will face increasingly severe penalties.
